Beautiful Chaos

A poem for B

You are the spark in a still sky,

The hush before the storm sighs.

A quiet that roars, soft but vast—

A present that outshines the past.

You're beautiful chaos, unrefined,

The art of a storm in a delicate mind.

I watch you spin, break, bloom, and heal—

A whirlwind that makes stillness feel.

And even when you pull away,

I see the moon in your quiet sway.

Even in silence, you still speak—

In your stillness, you’re never weak.

You’re a symphony without a score,

I’ll listen, love, and ask for more.

— Always yours, Carrots
